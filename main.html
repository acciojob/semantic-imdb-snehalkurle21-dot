Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who enters the subconscious mind through dreams to steal secrets. He is given a chance to redeem himself by planting an idea into a target’s mind.

Actors

The movie features Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Elliot Page, and Tom Hardy, each contributing to the film’s complex and engaging narrative.
